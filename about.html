About Me

My name is Conor Kotwasinski, and I am enrolled in the combined BS/MS program for computer science at Northwestern University. I enjoy the fields of cybersecurity and blockchain. Currently one of my CTF Team advisors and previous cybersecurity professors and I are working on a establishing a blockchain auditing firm.
